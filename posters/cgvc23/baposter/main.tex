	\begin{posterbox}[name=abstract,column=0,row=0, span=2]{Abstract}
		In this paper, we extend the scope of naming problem studies to encompass
		rule-based graph transformation modelling systems. We propose a novel
		persistent naming method that capitalizes on the formalized operations of
		generalized maps and graph transformation rules. It enables a unique and
		homogeneous characterisation of entities across all dimensions.
	\end{posterbox}

	\begin{posterbox}[name=conclusion,above=bottom]{Conclusion}
		\begin{itemize}
			\item Topology naming extension to rule based modelling systems
			\item Implementation of persistent naming scheme
			      \begin{itemize}
				      \item Unique identification of any topological cell
			      \end{itemize}
			\item Possibility to adapt modelling strategies
			      \begin{itemize}
				      \item Add topological parameters
				      \item Remove operations
