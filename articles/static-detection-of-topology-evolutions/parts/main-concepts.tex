A rule, written $r : L \longrightarrow R$, defines two schemes $L$ and $R$. $L$
describes a sub-graph to transform and $R$ a transformation of $L$.

\begin{definition}[Rule]
  Let $G$ be a Gmap and $H$ the transformation of $G$.

  A rule $r: L \longrightarrow R$ is defined by a couple of schemes $L$ and $R$.
  Its application on $G$ produces $H$.
\end{definition}
