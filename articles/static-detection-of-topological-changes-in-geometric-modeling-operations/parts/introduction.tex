\section[Introduction]{Introduction}
\label{sec:introduction}

For the last few decades, CAD softwares have been essentials tools for both general and specific purpose designs.
It allows to represent, for example, building, furniture or even high precision mechanical pieces.
These softwares rely on their geometric modeling kernel to provide the functionality needed to realize such tasks.
To provide users with good quality building boards and precisely describe models topological cells (vertex, edge, face, etc) changes, kernels require efficient topological changes tracking methods.
