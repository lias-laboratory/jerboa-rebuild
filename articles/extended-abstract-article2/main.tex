\begin{abstract}
In this paper, we extend the scope of naming problem studies to encompass rule-based graph transformation modeling systems. 
We propose a novel persistent naming method that capitalizes on the formalized operations of generalized maps and graph transformation rules. It enables a unique and homogeneous characterisation of entities across all dimensions.

\textbf{Keywords} : Topology-based modeling; Graph transformation rules; Persistent Naming; Reevaluation; Generalized maps;
\end{abstract}
